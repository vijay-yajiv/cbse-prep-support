Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the CBSE Class 10 Prep mobile application ("the App") handles your information. The App is developed and maintained by an individual developer and is available on the Apple App Store.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. Specifically:

  • We do not require account creation or login.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or other device sensors.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores your study progress, quiz scores, and preferences locally on your device using an on-device database (Hive). This data:

  • Never leaves your device.
  • Is not accessible to the developer or any third party.
  • Is removed when you uninstall the App.

3. In-App Purchases

The App may offer optional In-App Purchases processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not have access to your payment information, billing details, or Apple ID. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles payment data.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. No data is shared with third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is an educational tool designed for students preparing for CBSE Class 10 board examinations in India. Because the App does not collect any personal information, it is safe for use by children. We comply with applicable children's privacy regulations, including COPPA and India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and no data is transmitted over the internet, your information is protected by your device's own security measures (passcode, biometrics, encryption).

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. If significant changes are made, we will provide notice within the App.
