Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app work offline?

Yes! All questions, mock tests, and board papers are bundled with the app. No internet connection is needed to study.

Which subjects are covered?

Mathematics (Standard) and Science for CBSE Class 10, aligned with the latest NCERT syllabus.

Are the board papers from official CBSE sources?

Yes, all previous year question papers are sourced from the official CBSE website (cbse.gov.in).

How do I report a wrong answer?

If you spot an incorrect answer or explanation, please email us with the question ID (shown at the top of each question) and we'll fix it promptly.

Is there negative marking in mock tests?

No, just like the actual CBSE board exams for MCQs, there is no negative marking.

Can I request new features?

Absolutely! Email us your suggestions and we'll do our best to include them in future updates.
